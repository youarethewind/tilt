Tilt Prism

History of the font

Tilt Prism is a font that was created in 2019 by Andy Clymer. He was inspired by noticing the three dimensionality of the letters in a neon sign. Tilt Prism is one of three fonts in the Tilt Font family. This font has the ability to be rotated both horizontally and vertically.

Variations

Horizontal and vertical rotations up to +-45 degrees, so the font always maintains readability.
